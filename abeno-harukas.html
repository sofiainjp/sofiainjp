Located in the Abeno district of Osaka City, Abeno Harukas is the tallest building in Japan with 60 floors and a height of 300 meters, and the third tallest structure in Japan after the Tokyo Skytree (634m) and Tokyo Tower (332.6m).It is part of the Abeno Bridge Station building, which is co-located with Osaka Abeno Bridge Station on the Kintetsu Line.

The lower level houses the Abeno Harukas Kintetsu Main Store and the Harukas Museum of Art, the middle level houses offices, and the upper level houses the Osaka Marriott Hotel and the Harukas 300 observation deck.
