Karuizawa is a small town located in the eastern part of Nagano Prefecture. It is a famous summer resort with cool summers and comfortable living.

Kumoba Pond

Kumoba Pond is a long, narrow, man-made lake that is also known as Swan Lake because of the legend that white swans once stopped here.
It has been designated as a scenic spot in the Karuizawa area, and the entire area has been designated as the Kumoba Pond Scenic Area.

After renting a bicycle at the north exit of Karuizawa Station, you can follow the Google map and ride northwest for about 15 minutes through the alleys and trees to reach the most famous and popular scenic spot in Karuizawa, ‚ÄúKumoba Pond‚Äù.

Many people like to ride bicycles here because of the comfortable environment and the small number of cars on the road. It is recommended to rent a bicycle from the side of Karuizawa station and ride to Kumoba Pond and other attractions easily.

Karuizawa also has a wide selection of food, including Japanese kettle rice and set meals, as well as many Italian dishes, steak burgers, and light cafes.
