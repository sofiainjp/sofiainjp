Itsukushima Shrine

Sofia Chugoku Region (中国地方)

Itsukushima Shrine was built on the sea, so the view varies with the tide. When the tide level is 250cm or higher, the shrine looks like it is floating on the sea. If the tide is below 100cm, you can walk around the Ootorii. If you stay in the morning and evening on a day when the tide is not as high as it should be, you can experience both “floating in the sea” and “walking under the big torii”.。👉 👉Miyajima Tourist Association

When you come to Miyajima, don’t forget to eat the local food!～❤️

Raw Oysters
Local oysters are not only fresh and juicy, but they can be eaten in a variety of ways.Raw, grilled, fried and other cooking methods are available to satisfy the taste buds of gourmets.The only thing you have to worry about is whether you can handle the freshness of the sea.

Red Leaf Buns
The outside crust is similar to the texture of honey cake, and the shape is modeled after the leaves of the prefectural maple tree in Hiroshima.The filling is available in red bean, white bean, matcha, chestnut, cream, chocolate and cheese fillings.Ahh, it’s really delicious!

Travel Information

✔Take JR to “Miyajimaguchi” station first, then take the ferry.
✔When you get off at JR Miyajimaguchi Station, walk for five minutes and take the private Miyajimatsu Ferry or the JR Ferry to Miyajima! Both ferries run about 15 minutes apart, and the fare is 360 yen round trip, and the journey time is about 10 minutes.

Discount Ticket for foreign tourists

✔With the purchase of 1 Day Streetcar and Ferry Pass, you can take unlimited rides on the Hiroshima Electric Railway, a road train in Hiroshima City, and the Miyajimatsu Ferry, which takes you to Itsukushima Shrine and back。
💰Ticket price：Adults 900 yen，Children 450yen
