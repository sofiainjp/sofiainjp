KOKONOE“YUME”OTSURIHASH

Sofia Kyushu (九州)

KOKONOE“YUME”OTSURIHASH is 390 meters long, 173 meters high, 1.5 meters broad and it is the highest suspension bridge which is only for walkway in Japan.Right front of this bridge, you can see ‘Shindo-no-taki’ or ‘Medaki’which are selected as ‘100 beautiful waterfalls in Japan’.

Walking on the bridge, you can enjoy a 360 degree view, but it takes courage to challenge the 390 meter long, 173 meter high, 1.5 meter wide suspension bridge, and you need to hold your phone and camera steady.However, walking on the Skywalk and watching the magnificent scenery cleanses the body and mind.❤️

Although the leaves were not all red at the time of the photo shoot, you can imagine how touching it will be when all the leaves in the valley turn red.
If you visit Oita, you must take on this suspension bridge and see this magnificent view!

 

Visiting Information

Ticket/Time

Open (Last entry is 30 min. before closing time.)
[Jan.–Aug. / Nov.–Dec.]8:30am to 5:00pm
[Sep.– Oct.]8:30am to 6:00pm

💰Ticket price：Age over 13(over junior high student) 500 yen／Age 6–12 (elementary school student) 200 yen／Under age 6 free

Travel Information

Take the JR from Yufuin to “Bungo-Nakamura Station 豊後中村駅” and transfer to the bus. Get off at “KOKONOE“YUME”OTSURIHASH”.

💰Ticket price：Fares/One-way flat line Adults 500 yen ／Elementary school students 250 yen ／Preschoolers free (half price for people with disabilities)
