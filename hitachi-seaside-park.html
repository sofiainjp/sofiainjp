Hitachi Seaside Park

Sofia Kanto (Èñ¢Êù±)

Hitachi Seaside Park is a Japanese national park located in Hitaichinaka City, with amusement facilities and a flower garden.In spring, narcissuses, tulips and nemophila (baby blue eyes) attract lots of visitors. In early summer, poppies and roses, and in summer, zinnias and sunflowers, bloom. In autumn, you can enjoy kochia (summer cypress) and cosmos flowers. In winter, ice tulips can be seen, making for a rich complement of flora throughout the year.

Although shopping and seeing new things in Tokyo is fun and enjoyable, if you want to have a relaxing and healing trip, Hitachi Seaside Park is really a great place to take a walk and enjoy nature!

In addition, if you want to shop at Costco in Japan, there is also the Tsuru Naka Warehouse store nearby, just bring your Costco membership card, you can go shopping different from your country , there are many products available only in Japan!
